Our Courses




Undergraduate Courses

The location of your undergraduate studies is a significant yet exciting life decision. We believe that submitting an application to Robert Gordon University (RGU) is the best course of action for you for a number of reasons.

According to the National Student Survey from 2022, we are among the top 10 universities in the UK for overall student satisfaction*. We take pride in our student-centered philosophy and our inclusive and welcoming learning environment.
A report from the National Student Survey 2022, ranked Robert Gordon University second in Scotland for learning opportunities and learning communities, proving that we give our students a high-quality educational experience in which we continuously improve our curriculum in response to their needs. HESA Graduate Outcomes 2022, ranked RGU top in Scotland and third overall (outside of small, specialised institutions) for employability and/or further study. All of our undergraduate programmes are created to give you the skills and knowledge you need to succeed in your future professional careers.

We provide classes in many different subject areas, such as computing, art and design, creative industries, engineering, business, and law, as well as the health professions, architecture, and the built environment. For more information, entry requirements, and application instructions, visit the course pages.
*Excluding colleges, private, specialised, and small businesses.

Postgraduate Courses

Whether you have recently graduated, want professional accreditation, are looking to retrain or develop your career- whatever your reasons for postgraduate study - we offer a wide range of courses with flexible study modes including online, part-time or full-time to fit around your needs.

Robert Gordon University provides 105 postgraduate degree options in a variety of different courses to fit any indiviudals needs.

Online Learning Courses

More than 5000 students have enrolled in an online degree programme in the past five years! We offer more than 100 online degrees for you to choose from, so you can study at your convenience whether you're looking to upgrade your skills, advance in your career, or simply expand your knowledge in a new subject area.

Our virtual learning environment CampusMoodle, which enables full and open access to your tutors, relevant industry professionals, and other online students around the world, is used to deliver our online courses, ensuring a positive and worthwhile learning experience for you. You will be taught using a variety of media types, such as online lectures, podcasts, and tutorials. With our approach to online learning, you are in charge and can schedule your studies around your obligations at work and in your personal life.



About Our University







Who We Are

Robert Gordon University is an innovative, inclusive, impactful and professionally-focused institution focused on demand-led teaching and research

RGU offers students a relevant curriculum, industry standard tools and a high-quality vocational education that equips students with the skills they need to succeed in their careers. The university has a reputation for turning out highly sought-after graduates as a result.
RGU has consistently had one of the best records of any UK university for graduate level employment over the past ten years. It is currently ranked among Scotland's top three universities for student satisfaction, and it celebrates a high calibre student experience. According to the Guardian University Guide 2023, it has been ranked as a Top University for Satisfaction and Employability.



RGU was granted university status in 1992 and has a 250-year history. The university has remained dedicated to fostering equal access to a useful and relevant education throughout its development.
It consists of eleven schools and provides more than 300 courses in subjects like business, health and social care, engineering, architecture, computing, and life sciences. It is one of the largest providers of online learning in the UK, with over 16,500 students studying both on-campus and online.
In order to address regional and national skill needs, courses are developed in close cooperation with employers, professions, and industry. This guarantees that the curriculum is demand-driven.



The university is investing heavily to expand its globally significant research in important fields like industrial biotechnology, smart data and artificial intelligence, pharmacy practise, and sustainable transportation. The university fosters excellence in knowledge exchange and interdisciplinary collaboration by giving priority to strategic research partnerships.
RGU has a strong history in workforce development on the national and international levels and is renowned for its close relationships with industry.
To promote innovation and propel economic development, it works with organisations, governments, trade bodies, industry associations, and academic institutions.
